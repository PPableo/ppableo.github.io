A Modern Photobooth Website for Capturing Memories

Photohaus is an all-age modern photobooth service designed to capture special moments at events, making them unforgettable. This website serves as a digital storefront for a first-time business owner, attracting clients and showcasing their unique offerings.

Features

  • Built using Next.js and Tailwind CSS for a modern, responsive design
  • Framer Motion integration for smooth, engaging animations
  • Multi-page structure including homepage, packages, testimonials, and gallery
  • Interactive gallery with categorized events and click-to-enlarge functionality
  • FAQ section with accordion-style display for easy navigation
  • Templates page showcasing various photobooth designs
  • Seamless integration with external booking system

Design

The Photohaus website embodies a perfect blend of fun and professionalism. With a vibrant color scheme and playful typography, it immediately conveys the excitement of a photobooth experience. The responsive design ensures a seamless experience across all devices, while the intuitive navigation guides potential clients through services, pricing, and booking processes with ease. High-quality images and animated elements showcase the photobooth in action, giving visitors a taste of the memories they could create.
